# Research Report

## Research Question
What are the real-world risks and benefits of using synthetic data to train large language models?

## Key Benefits

1. **Synthetic data is transforming AI development by addressing data scarcity and improving privacy.**
   - Evidence: Healthcare and finance are leveraging it to address data scarcity and improve privacy.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

2. **Synthetic data is set to dominate AI models by 2030.**
   - Evidence: Synthetic data is projected to surpass real data in AI models by 2030.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

3. **Synthetic data is 100 times cheaper to generate than real data.**
   - Evidence: It's 100 times cheaper to generate than real data.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

4. **Synthetic data enhances privacy by safeguarding sensitive information.**
   - Evidence: It safeguards sensitive information in sectors like healthcare and finance.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

5. **Synthetic data helps overcome challenges like privacy violations and data collection costs.**
   - Evidence: It enables AI development without the ethical and logistical hurdles of real data collection.
   - [Source](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/synthetic-data-generation-enhancing-ai-training-datasets-patil-d3qpe)

6. **Synthetic data can improve model generalization by including rare cases.**
   - Evidence: Designed to cover rare cases, synthetic data enhances generalization, crucial for applications like autonomous driving.
   - [Source](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/synthetic-data-generation-enhancing-ai-training-datasets-patil-d3qpe)

7. **High-fidelity synthetic data generated by larger models is necessary to teach small models to reason like large ones.**
   - Evidence: To teach a small model to reason like a large one, you need high-fidelity synthetic data generated by larger models.
   - [Source](https://huggingface.co/blog/rishiraj/challenges-of-synthetic-dataset-generation)

## Key Risks

1. **Synthetic data can conceal real-world disparities.**
   - Evidence: Synthetic data can conceal real-world disparities, necessitating constant monitoring of its use.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

2. **Synthetic data poses re-identification risks.**
   - Evidence: Advanced generative models might inadvertently reveal personal information.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

3. **Legal and ethical frameworks are needed to regulate synthetic data usage.**
   - Evidence: The lack of standard guidelines for generating synthetic data demands additional protective measures.
   - [Source](https://keymakr.com/blog/ethical-and-legal-considerations-of-synthetic-data-usage/)

4. **Creating synthetic data that is fair and representative is a complex task.**
   - Evidence: Creating synthetic data that is both fair and representative is a complex task.
   - [Source](https://www.york.ac.uk/news-and-events/news/2025/research/synthetic-data/)

5. **Quality assurance is a challenge in synthetic data generation.**
   - Evidence: Ensuring synthetic data reflects real-world distributions is critical; poor quality can lead to models failing in real-world scenarios.
   - [Source](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/synthetic-data-generation-enhancing-ai-training-datasets-patil-d3qpe)

6. **Bias in generation can perpetuate biases in models.**
   - Evidence: Generative methods must be bias-free; training on biased real data can perpetuate biases, requiring careful design.
   - [Source](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/synthetic-data-generation-enhancing-ai-training-datasets-patil-d3qpe)

## Evaluation and Quality Concerns

1. **Synthetic data have significant and disruptive ethical implications.**
   - Evidence: Synthetic data intervene in our understanding of long-standing issues such as bias, fairness, and algorithmic injustice.
   - [Source](https://www.york.ac.uk/news-and-events/news/2025/research/synthetic-data/)

2. **The area of synthetic data remains both under-researched and under-theorised.**
   - Evidence: Although there is a substantive literature about the effects of algorithms on society, the area of synthetic data remains both under-researched and under-theorised.
   - [Source](https://www.york.ac.uk/news-and-events/news/2025/research/synthetic-data/)

3. **Models trained on synthetic data need thorough validation against real data to ensure performance.**
   - Evidence: This step is often debated for its complexity.
   - [Source](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/synthetic-data-generation-enhancing-ai-training-datasets-patil-d3qpe)

4. **Batch degradation results in decreased quality of synthetic data as more examples are generated in a single pass.**
   - Evidence: The first 10 examples are usually excellent. Around example 20, the creativity dips.
   - [Source](https://huggingface.co/blog/rishiraj/challenges-of-synthetic-dataset-generation)

## Summary
The use of synthetic data in training large language models presents a dual-edged sword of benefits and risks. On one hand, it offers significant advantages such as cost-effectiveness, enhanced privacy, and the ability to address data scarcity and improve model generalization. On the other hand, it raises ethical concerns regarding bias, representation, and the quality of generated data. As synthetic data continues to evolve, it is crucial to establish robust legal and ethical frameworks to mitigate risks while maximizing its potential benefits in AI development.
